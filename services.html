About Me

Once upon a time in the lively city of Manchester, I, Abi, found myself navigating the corridors of healthcare as a dedicated nurse in general medicine. Amidst the constant hustle of hospitals, I discovered a latent passion that would soon shape my destiny – a desire for both healing and innovation.Driven by this newfound yearning, I enrolled in a web development course at Code Institute. Juggling my nursing duties during the day and coding aspirations during the night, I embraced the challenge wholeheartedly. Hospital breaks became coding sessions, and the empathy I carried for patients transformed into lines of code.

As the digital world unfolded before me, I emerged not just as a nurse but as AK, the web developer. My skills seamlessly blended medical precision with digital finesse, creating a unique identity in the heart of Manchester. The city, known for its industrial spirit, became the canvas for my creative aspirations. Venturing into entrepreneurship, I established "AK Web Solutions" – a reflection of my distinctive blend of medical expertise and coding prowess. My website, mirroring Manchester's dynamic energy, proudly showcased services that went beyond the ordinary.

Clients resonated with my approach, and their testimonials echoed their satisfaction. One client praised, "AK doesn't just build websites; she crafts digital experiences that reflect the heart of Manchester. Her background in nursing brings a human touch to every project." Another testimonial exclaimed, "Choosing AK was a game-changer for our business. Her perspective as a nurse turned coder brought a refreshing approach to our project. Our website not only functions flawlessly but also resonates with the spirit of Manchester." The symphony of my success reverberated through the city, as businesses flourished under my guidance. The keyboard became my instrument, playing a melody that harmonized with the heartbeat of Manchester. In this digital landscape, I found my true calling – a web developer whose work not only transformed businesses but also enriched the vibrant tapestry of Manchester's online presence.
